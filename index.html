About me

I’m an executor and product person. With more than 10 years in tech and product, I’ve spent the past four years in product leadership:

  • CPO at Wagestream, London-based B2B2C fintech providing fairer financial services for all; global leader in Earned Wage Access. Currently Series B.
  • CPO at bloomon, Amsterdam-based high-end retail consumer subscription scale-up with a proprietary supply chain and tech platform. Acquired by Bloom&Wild in 2021.
  • Director of Product at Skyscanner, Edinburgh-based travel marketplace and global leader for flight metasearch. Acquired by Ctrip in 2016.

The common threads were:

  • Delivering top-line business impact
  • Scaling global products internationally
  • Re-igniting growth in scaling companies (avoiding plateaus!)
  • Building best-in-class product organisations and high-performing teams
  • Attracting and developing top talent

Originally from Alaska and with stints in Washington, D.C. and Amsterdam, I currently live in Edinburgh and split my free time between the sea, the mountains and books.
